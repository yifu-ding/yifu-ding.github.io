About Me

Yifu Ding is currently a PhD student (2021.9-) in the State Key Laboratory of Software Development Environment (SKLSDE) and Shen Yuan Honors College at Beihang University under the supervision of Prof. Xianglong Liu. Since 2020, she devoted to model compression via parameter quantization and enjoyed working with the responsible tutor and talented team members. Under the trend of large models in artificial intellegence, she is now still diving in model quantization which can speed up the inference and reduce the consumption of storaget and power resources when implemented on edge devices.
