I am a PhD student (2021.9-) in School of Computer Science and Engineering at Beihang University and and Shen Yuan Honors College at Beihang University under the supervision of Prof. Xianglong Liu. My research interest is model compression and acceleration, especially using quantization approaches. Under the trend of large models in deep learning, quantization can speed up the inference and reduce the consumption of storage and power resources when implemented on edge devices. I enjoy working with the responsible tutor and talented team members, and look forward to any possible cooperations.
